Catdow

(This post is on-hold of writing)

Details:

  • A 3D game inspired by Lara Croft GO
  • Game project 2.
  • 6-week development with a wonderful team of 5 programmers, 3 level designers and 4 artists.
  • Game is made in Unity.
  • The game is targeting mobile-platform and developed for android os.
  • https://carrydev.itch.io/catdow 

 

My contributions:

  • Swipe System (Mouse and Touch-screen)
  • Character movements (tile-based & turn based)
  • Animation Controller for Player and Flytrap
  • Pushing & Pulling Crate System
  • Cauldron System to escape damage from light sources
